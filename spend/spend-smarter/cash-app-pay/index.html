Pay Your Way with Cash App Pay

A faster, safer way to pay online and in stores with exclusive rewards.

Cash App Pay is revolutionizing how you make payments, offering a seamless way to pay both online and in stores while earning exclusive rewards.

How Cash App Pay Works

Cash App Pay lets you make payments using your Cash App balance or linked payment methods. Simply scan a QR code in stores or select Cash App Pay at checkout online.

In-Store Payments

Scan the merchant's QR code to pay instantly

Online Checkout

Select Cash App Pay at participating online stores

Getting Started

  1. Update Cash App to the latest version
  2. Ensure you have funds in your Cash App balance
  3. Look for the Cash App Pay option at checkout
  4. Scan QR code or select Cash App Pay online
  5. Confirm payment with your PIN or Touch ID

Rewards and Benefits

Every purchase with Cash App Pay can earn you rewards through Boosts. Select from available Boosts to save on your next purchase.

Security Features

  • Encrypted transactions
  • PIN or biometric authentication
  • Real-time fraud monitoring
  • Instant purchase notifications

Where to Use Cash App Pay

Cash App Pay is accepted at a growing number of retailers, both online and in stores. Look for the Cash App Pay logo or QR code at checkout.

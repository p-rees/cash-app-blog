Understanding Buy Now, Pay Later

Everything you need to know about Afterpay on Cash App, from how it works to managing payments responsibly.

Sarah Chen May 1, 2025
8 min read

What is Buy Now, Pay Later?

Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) is a payment option that lets you split your purchase into four equal installments, paid over six weeks. With Cash App's integration of Afterpay, you can use this feature at millions of merchants while managing your payments directly in the app you already use.

How Afterpay Works on Cash App

When you make a purchase with Afterpay on Cash App, your payment is split into four equal parts:

  • First payment (25%) is due at the time of purchase
  • Second payment (25%) is due 2 weeks later
  • Third payment (25%) is due 4 weeks after purchase
  • Final payment (25%) is due 6 weeks after purchase

"Afterpay has helped me budget better by spreading out payments for larger purchases. It's like having a built-in payment plan, but without the interest or hidden fees."

Benefits of Using Afterpay

Interest-Free Payments

Unlike credit cards, Afterpay doesn't charge any interest on your purchases. As long as you make your payments on time, you'll only pay the original purchase price, split into four equal payments.

Easy Integration

Afterpay is built right into your Cash App, making it easy to:

  • View your payment schedule
  • Make payments
  • Track your spending
  • Set up automatic payments

Using Afterpay Responsibly

While Buy Now, Pay Later can be a helpful tool, it's important to use it responsibly. Here are some tips:

  • Only make purchases you can afford
  • Keep track of multiple payment schedules
  • Set up automatic payments to avoid late fees
  • Consider your overall budget and other financial commitments

Getting Started with Afterpay

Ready to try Afterpay? Here's how to get started:

  1. Update your Cash App to the latest version
  2. Look for the Afterpay option when shopping at participating merchants
  3. Select Afterpay at checkout
  4. Review and confirm your payment schedule
  5. Complete your purchase

Your First 30 Days with Cash App

A comprehensive guide to getting started with Cash App, from setup to your first month of use. Learn the essential features, tips, and tricks to make the most of your Cash App experience.

12 min read

Getting Started with Cash App

Welcome to Cash App! Whether you're new to digital banking or looking to make the most of your financial tools, this guide will walk you through your first month with Cash App. We'll cover everything from basic setup to advanced features, helping you become a Cash App pro in just 30 days.

Days 1-7: Setting Up Your Account

Day 1: Download and Sign Up

  • Download Cash App from the App Store or Google Play
  • Sign up with your phone number or email
  • Choose your unique $Cashtag
  • Link your debit card for instant transfers

Days 2-3: Send Your First Payment

  • Send a small test payment to a friend
  • Learn how to request money
  • Explore payment notes and emojis

"Start small with a test payment to a friend. It's the best way to get comfortable with sending and receiving money digitally."

Days 4-7: Get Your Cash Card

  • Order your free Cash Card
  • Customize your card design
  • Download the QR code for easy payments
  • Set up your PIN for ATM access

Days 8-14: Explore Money Features

Days 8-10: Add Money to Your Balance

  • Link your bank account
  • Set up direct deposit
  • Learn about instant deposits
  • Try adding paper money at a store

Days 11-14: Discover Cash Card Boosts

  • Activate your first Boost
  • Learn how to switch between Boosts
  • Track your savings
  • Explore available merchants

Days 15-21: Advanced Features

Days 15-17: Banking Features

  • Set up direct deposit
  • Get your account and routing numbers
  • Learn about getting paid early

Days 18-21: Security Features

  • Enable Face ID/Touch ID
  • Set up Security Lock
  • Add a backup payment method
  • Review privacy settings

"Security should be your top priority. Take time to set up all available security features to protect your money."

Days 22-30: Becoming a Pro User

Days 22-25: Investing (Optional)

  • Learn about stocks and Bitcoin
  • Set up your investing profile
  • Try Auto-Invest with spare change

Days 26-30: Advanced Tips

  • Set up recurring payments
  • Explore business features
  • Learn about Cash App Pay
  • Connect with support resources

Tips for Success

  • Start with small transactions until you're comfortable
  • Enable notifications to track activity
  • Keep your app updated for the latest features
  • Save frequently used contacts as favorites
  • Review your activity feed regularly

What's Next?

After your first 30 days, you'll have a solid foundation with Cash App. Continue exploring new features as they're released, and make the most of your Cash Card Boosts to save money on everyday purchases. Remember to check the app regularly for new offers and features that can help you manage your money better.
